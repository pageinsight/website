PAGE INSIGHT

Simple and insightful alternative to Google Analytics

Page Insight is a privacy-friendly, lightweight and open-source website analytics tool. No cookies and fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA and PECR.

It's time to ditch Google Analytics

Web analytics went from a simple, fun and useful practice for site owners to a data grabbing machine for surveillance capitalism. Google Analytics is frustrating to use, difficult to understand, slow to load and privacy-invasive too.

Page insight is built for privacy conscious site owners. You get actionable stats to help you improve your efforts while your visitors keep having a nice and enjoyable experience.

Our script is 50 times smaller than the Google Analytics script. Your page size will be cut down and your site will load faster.

Page Insight is easy to use

Page insight is simple analytics. It is easy to understand and it cuts throught the noise. Check your site traffic and get all the essential insights you need to improve your site. There is no need for PowerPoint documents.

Page Insight is powerful. Segment the data by any metric you click on. Answer the important questions about your visitors, content and referral sources. Analyze paid campaigns and unknown traffic using UTM parameters. Create custom events, see how they convert over time and identify trends that matter.

Page Insight is open source analytics. Our source code is available and accessible on GitHub so anyone can read it, inspect it and review it to verify that our actions match with our words. We welcome feedback and have a responsive support team.
